Rotating Wall Frame with Computer Graphics

For my computer graphics class, I had to create a wall frame for a homework assignment. We had to build the wall frame using different transformations on a unit 1x1x1 block of "wood". I had to instantiate each 2x4 and then transform it to the correct position. After I completed that, I added rotation to easily view the wall frame.
